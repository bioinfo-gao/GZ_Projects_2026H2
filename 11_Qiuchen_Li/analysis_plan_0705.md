## 6. Key Parameters & Rationale

- **Comparison design:** All 3 pairwise contrasts (Mix vs NT, A5BKO vs NT, A5BKO vs Mix) ‚Äî confirmed with user; no single reference group.
- **Gene filter:** GENCODE `gene_type == protein_coding` via `human_Gene_annotation` xlsx (falls back to regex if xlsx unavailable).
- **Low-expression filter:** ‚â•10 counts in ‚â•(n‚àí2) samples.
- **Significance threshold:** padj ‚â§ 0.05 & |log2FC| ‚â• 0.263 (log2(1.2), ‚â•1.2-fold).
- **LFC shrinkage:** ashr.
- **No mouse-specific STAR twopass override** ‚Äî human aligns with nf-core defaults.

## 8. Known Limitations

- Only 3 replicates per group ‚Äî adequate for DESeq2 but limits detection of small-effect DEGs.
- 3-way pairwise design increases multiple-testing burden vs a single-control design; each contrast's padj is independently BH-corrected within that contrast.
